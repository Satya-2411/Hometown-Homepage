Top 3 activities to do at Hyderabad

Explore the Iconic Charminar

Step into the heart of Old Hyderabad — admire the majestic Charminar

Savor Authentic Hyderabadi Biryani

Indulge in the city’s signature dish at Paradise, Bawarchi, or Shadab. The rich aroma and royal flavors make it an unforgettable experience.

Discover the Grandeur of Golconda Fort

Walk through centuries of history at Golconda Fort, and don’t miss the evening light and sound show that brings its glorious past to life.
